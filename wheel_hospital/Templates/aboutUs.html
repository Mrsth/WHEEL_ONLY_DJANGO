About Us

Who are we ?

Wheeler’s Hospital, is a web based application developed for providing vehicle servicing and maintenance facilities where an individual doesn't need to visit the servicing center to get their vehicle serviced or maintained. We are committed for providing a better bike servicing facility via a web application.

Our motive

We aim to meet the following objectives:

  • Providing proper online vehicle servicing facility.
  • Saving customer’s time for visiting the servicing center.
  • Vehicle pick up and delivery as per customer’s convenient location.
